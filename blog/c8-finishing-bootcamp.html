Finishing Bootcamp

After 18 weeks of intense coding at EDA, Horoeka managed to finish an MVP of our web app called Feedr. The final stage of the EDA bootcamp saw us completing a week long group project using all of the skills we’d learned over the last 18 weeks.
Our goal was to make a web app using React and Redux that was functional and ready to be presented to an audience at the end of the week. We brainstormed a multitude of ideas, but we decided to create an app that helps businesses give excess food to charities. We called it Feedr and started to plan our masterpiece!
